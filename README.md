# 🚴 Swiggy Food Delivery Performance Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive, business-intelligence style Power BI dashboard built to evaluate food delivery logistics, restaurant profitability, financial health trends, and customer satisfaction metrics for Swiggy operations. 

This project bridges operations management with commercial analytics to uncover regional market expansion strategies and customer lifetime value optimizations.

---

---

### 💡 Data Insights & Business Metrics
* **Financial Volatility & Cost Efficiency:** Monthly gross sales vs. cost tracking reveals a heavy revenue drop-off mid-year, dipping significantly to a floor in **June ($0.34M Gross Sales / $0.20M Cost)** before rallying strongly to close the fiscal calendar in **December ($0.75M Gross Sales / $0.22M Cost)**[cite: 2].
* **Discount Efficiency vs. Net Profit ROI:** Heavy discount spending spiked during core quarters (peaking in January at $119K and December at $116K) but showed highly efficient profit conversion ($219K and $217K respectively), proving that promotional strategies heavily drive top-line growth[cite: 2].
* **Restaurant Performance Tiering:** **South Delight** stands as the anchor enterprise restaurant, clearing **$233K** in standalone profits, followed closely by **Food Palace ($206K)** and **Spice Hub ($192K)**[cite: 2].
* **Market Capitalization by City:** **Bangalore** is the primary dominant operational hub clearing **$106K** in profit metrics, followed sequentially by Kolkata ($109K), Chennai ($114K), Mumbai ($47K), Delhi ($39K), Pune, and Hyderabad[cite: 2].
* **Customer Retention Mechanics:** Quadrant mapping of *Customer Satisfaction vs. Average Order Value* isolates that **Pizza Corner** generates the highest average customer satisfaction score (~3.05 rating) with strong order values ($500+), while **Biryani House** maps lower on satisfaction but maintains highly resilient purchase sizing[cite: 2].
* **Logistics & Cuisine Distribution:** Market demand remains incredibly evenly split across the platform, with **South Indian (21.18%)** holding the slight edge over Italian (20.16%), Fast Food (19.98%), Chinese (19.53%), and North Indian (19.14%)[cite: 2].

### 🛠️ Technical Features & Architecture
* **Advanced Multi-Axis Forecasting:** Implemented dual-axis charts overlaying discount capital outlays against net revenue trends across dynamic monthly variables[cite: 2].
* **Scatter Plot Cohort Segmentation:** Integrated metric distribution matrices tracking the overlap between customer review values against economic transaction sizing[cite: 2].
* **Payment Funnel Breakdown:** Visualized channel transaction splits highlighting volume routing across **UPI**, **Debit Cards**, **Credit Cards**, **Cash**, and **Digital Wallets**[cite: 2].
* **Data Cleansing Pipeline:** Structured row-level logistics data handling datetime intervals (calculating minutes metrics) and locale conversions.
